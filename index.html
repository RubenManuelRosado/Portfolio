Kasa Vacation Planner

My latest independent project, volunteering to create a value proposition for Kasa.com. Kasa Living, Inc. is a real estate technology company that transforms underutilized real estate into stylishly-furnished, professionally managed units that cater to business and leisure travelers seeking trustworthy and comfortable accommodations.

The purpose of the Kasa Vacation Planner is to provide value to the booking process by enabling website visitors to validate trip feasibility against an accrued vacation analysis.

Using Excel VBA with Selenium, I captured availability data and iteratively created all available "trips." Each trip has a start and end date, and the trip's vacation days are just the number of weekdays during the trip. The program automatically excludes trips that require more vacation days than the user would have accrued at the time of the trip. It also gives users the ability to maximize their vacation usage by highlighting trips with multi-weekend availability.

Accelerated Debt Payoff Calculator

This project is close to my heart. I want to help people free themselves from the chains of debt, so they can do good with their money rather than giving it away as loan interest.

I've been using Mint.com for over a decade to track my own personal finances. Over the years, I've learned that it just doesn't serve me. The world needs better tools. That's why I've created a unique debt payoff calculator helping clients get out of debt fast.

The premise - If you meet the budget you set, you can project your income and expenses into the future. You will know with certainty how much money you would have every day of the foreseeable future. As a result, you'll know exactly how much extra money you can use to pay down debt on the day that payments are due while still having money for future budgeted expenses and emergencies.

Using this projection method, my clients have found themselves debt-free 14 years sooner than previously expected, saving tens of thousands of dollars on interest payments and bringing peace of mind to their lives.

Pro Ball Analytics

This was a fun project back in 2017 where I first attempted to apply my skills as an entrepreneur.

The Fantasy Sports community was desperate to find a way to qualify their draft picks using meaningful data. Utilizing Excel VBA, Access VBA, Win Automation, Tableau, and Zappier, I was able to automate the creation of a large flat file dataset that enabled users to get the most bang for their buck. The filtering capabilities allowed lineups to be backfilled with qualified, cost-effective draft picks.
